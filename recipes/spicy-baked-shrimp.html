Spicy Baked Shrimp

About this Recipe

Spicy baked shrimp is a tantalizing dish that combines succulent shrimp with bold and fiery flavors. Typically, shrimp are marinated in a spicy mixture of seasonings such as chili powder, cayenne pepper, garlic, paprika, and cumin, along with olive oil or butter for moisture. The shrimp are then arranged on a baking sheet and roasted in the oven until they turn pink and slightly crispy on the edges.


This cooking method intensifies the flavors and ensures that the shrimp remain juicy and tender. Spicy baked shrimp can be served as an appetizer, main course, or even added to salads or pasta dishes for an extra kick of flavor. It's a quick and easy dish that's sure to impress with its vibrant taste and satisfying heat.
